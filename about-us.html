Who We Are

Siroccoi (U) Limited is a leading engineering company based in Jinja, Uganda, specializing in project management, consultancy, and supply chain solutions. Our expertise spans various sectors, including industrial and construction services for hydro, mechanical, and chemical plants. We are committed to exceeding client expectations through quality work, flexibility, and state-of-the-art equipment.

Our Mission

To conduct public and private construction works with quality, trust, and respect for the environment, while serving clients professionally.

Our Vision

We aim to enhance our domestic reputation, achieve sustainable growth, boost customer confidence, improve productivity, and strengthen our safety, health, and environmental policies.
